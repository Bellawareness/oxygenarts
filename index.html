About Michael Griffith

Michael Griffith is the artist and proprietor behind Oxygen Arts Guyana, a business centered on art and wellbeing. With almost 15 years of professional artistic practice, Michael is proficient in both traditional and digital media, displaying remarkable versatility spanning hyper-realistic portraits, caricatures, book illustrations, concept art, and commercial art.

The 39-year-old has won first prize at several national art competitions and studied at both the Burrows School of Art and the University of Guyana, where he graduated at the top of his class. His works have been exhibited both nationally and internationally and are often described as thought-provoking.

Apart from being an exceptional visual artist, Michael is also a mental health practitioner with a passion for personal growth and wellbeing. He graduated from the American University of Peace Studies with a degree in psychology and is currently completing his master's in clinical psychology.

About Our Approach

Michael Griffith believes that art and psychology are complementary disciplines, where one helps the individual to better understand the self, and the other helps to refine the expressive process. With his wealth of knowledge and experience as both a visual artist and mental health practitioner, he offers integrated services combining art therapy, meditation, and hypnotherapy.

Whether you're seeking stress relief, personal growth, or healing from past experiences, Michael is here to guide you on your wellness journey in a safe, supportive environment built on compassion and professional expertise.
